Sponsors

We are grateful for the support from our sponsors for the festival of India. On behalf of the 150 volunteers, 1,000+ participants and 10,000 to 20,000 visitors to the annual Festival, we express our thanks and deep appreciation. Sponsorship helps defer the cost of the festival, keeping ticket prices low, and enabling thousands of attendees the opportunity to enjoy the festival at a low price.

There are several levels of sponsorship available. Click the links on the Sponsorship Package for more details.
