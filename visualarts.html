The festival showcases one of the largest exhibits of local artists and their work. A variety of artistic media is on display. Exhibits include art on canvas, framed art, vases, and more. The goal is to give our local talent the freedom to express their opinions and thoughts.A special section is dedicated to our young artists to show case their work.

If you are interested in exhibiting your work at the Festival of India, click below to register.

Films and Documentaries

Objective: We look for film makers interested in cross cultural issues who have developed short films or documentaries on the cultural cross currents of the Indian Diaspora around the world. And offer a selected number of them a platform to present their work to a receptive audience of visitors to the festival.

Kids Art Corner and Competiton

We invite children to participate in creative expression with help from experienced artists. The end product will be an artifact with an Indian-American theme. No registration is required to participate in the Kids Art Corner.

We encourage kids seriously interested in visual arts, to participate in an Arts competition on themes related to Indian American cultural experience, and recognize the top winners.
